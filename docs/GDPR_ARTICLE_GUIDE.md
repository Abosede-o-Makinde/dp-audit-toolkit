# GDPR Article Guide

## Article 5: Data Principles

Personal data must be processed lawfully, fairly, transparently, accurately, for limited purposes, and securely.

## Article 25: Privacy by Design

Build privacy into systems from the start.

## Article 32: Security Measures

Implement appropriate security controls.

## Article 33: Breach Notification

Notify ICO within 72 hours of a breach.

## Article 35: DPIA

Assess impacts of high-risk processing.

## Article 37: DPO Designation

Appoint a Data Protection Officer if required.
